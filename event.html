As part of promoting the entrepreneurship culture among students, the St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai( SJCET PALAI), in association with Startup Village and the Kerala Government, has decided to set up a Startup Boot Camp at the college. The Startup Boot Camp is a novel project envisaged by the Kerala Government for creating a student network at the grassroots level to promote the entrepreneurship culture among students, and to familiarise them with the process of running business.
